My name is Juana Alvarez, I am an Argentine graphic designer. I studied at the University of Buenos Aires (FADU) and from a very young age I began to work in design agency in the City of Buenos Aires. After finishing my studies I began to travel until I reached my final and current destination, my mother’s hometown in Florence-Italy.

I have been working for more than a decade for a company called Cantiere Creativo, which is mainly dedicated to Web products, but which also has design projects. Thanks to its contacts with famous entities such as The Uffizi Gallery, I had the opportunity to carry out projects of great importance such us the Wayfinding for one of the most important museums in the world. At the same time, I carried out personal projects as a freelance related to different areas of design, mostly Branding and Packaging. I am currently studying Frontend Development with the Coder House school, my goal is to reduce the gap that exists today between a designer and a programmer, which will allow me to carry out web design projects in a more fluid and friendly way. I will continue training and learning code and maybe one day I will be able to carry out these projects not only focusing on the UX but also creating the layout in HTML and CSS.

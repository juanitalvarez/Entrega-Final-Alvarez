Wayfinding Boboli Gardens - Florence

  • Wayfinding
  • 2018
  • Culture & Arts

We had the opportunity to collaborate further with the Uffizi Galleries , focusing on the wayfinding of the Boboli Gardens. For years the Garden has had simple signage, which could not clearly show points of interest, indicating the best way, wasting visitors' time and energy. The first preparatory activity for signage was the conception and design of a clear and easy-to-read map, which told everything you need to know about the Garden before and during the visit.

Once the map was created, the real wayfinding work began, studying the most effective methods to help visitors find the points of interest, while explaining the travel times and the gradients, thus saving energy. It was also crucial to make clear and legible the position of all available services (toilets, fountains, panoramic points, etc.).

SOLUTION
Cantiere Creative was asked to create a brand identity to reflect the vibrant vision of Oriente Occidente that represents their spirit and vision. The new identity has the scope and flexibility to be at home in reference to any type of performance art: it can be exuberant and creatively playful, or intelligent, serious and institutional when necessary. Geometric shapes from logo simplification are the unifying symbol across all brand platforms and expressions, from posters and show materials to animated digital ads and social media. Shapes can be reconfigured to perform within the layout and interact with the photograph, or become a container of images. Identity can also serve as a surrounding framework for individual show graphics.

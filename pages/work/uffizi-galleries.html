Uffizi Galleries — Web Design

  • Web Design
  • 2018
  • Culture

Since 2014, the Uffizi Galleries have brought together three extraordinary museum complexes which together house the nucleus of the collections of art and precious handicrafts, books and botany of the Medici, Hapsburg, Lorraine and Savoy families: an authentic collection of treasures from Antiquity to the 20th century which have made the name of the Uffizi, Palazzo Pitti and the Boboli Gardens great over time; three museum sites brought together and connected to each other, since the Renaissance, through the ingenious construction of the Vasari Corridor, and which together constitute one of the most important and most visited cultural poles in the world.

A cut with the past, or a cut for the future An epochal event for the Uffizi Galleries: a new brand, created by CarmieUbertis, and a new website. A real media revolution. The apparent simplicity of the site is the result of a careful study of information architecture which opens up multiple avenues for the evolution of this editorial platform. A system designed and created ad hoc with excellent technologies to relate the different targets in a transversal way with the numerous content areas. Thus the static sections we are used to disappear, to give life to dynamic structures that evolve and are enriched day after day.

SOLUTION
Cantiere Creative was asked to create a brand identity to reflect the vibrant vision of Oriente Occidente that represents their spirit and vision. The new identity has the scope and flexibility to be at home in reference to any type of performance art: it can be exuberant and creatively playful, or intelligent, serious and institutional when necessary. Geometric shapes from logo simplification are the unifying symbol across all brand platforms and expressions, from posters and show materials to animated digital ads and social media. Shapes can be reconfigured to perform within the layout and interact with the photograph, or become a container of images. Identity can also serve as a surrounding framework for individual show graphics.

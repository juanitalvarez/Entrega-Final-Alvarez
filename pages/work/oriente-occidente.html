Re Branding - Oriente Occidente

  • Branding
  • 2022
  • Culture

Oriente Occidente is a cultural association that believes in the practice and culture of dialogue and plans networks of relationships through the language of dance, which by its definition goes beyond the boundaries: of language, ethnicity, gender, age, culture. With a visionary and excellent look. It is a bridge between East and West, between North and South, between form and content, between ethics and aesthetics, between the past and the future.

REQUEST
We were contacted to redesign the Oriente Occidente brand. They had a historic brand, created in 1981 by the Italian designer "Duilio Martinis", to which they were very close and which they did not want to distort, but at the same time they knew they had some gaps in their overall branding. There wasn't a coherent style that connected all their communication and there was also the need to create a brand book that established guidelines that helped to have a single vision of their graphic universe, as well as a clear message to communicate to the world all projects.

SOLUTION
Cantiere Creative was asked to create a brand identity to reflect the vibrant vision of Oriente Occidente that represents their spirit and vision. The new identity has the scope and flexibility to be at home in reference to any type of performance art: it can be exuberant and creatively playful, or intelligent, serious and institutional when necessary. Geometric shapes from logo simplification are the unifying symbol across all brand platforms and expressions, from posters and show materials to animated digital ads and social media. Shapes can be reconfigured to perform within the layout and interact with the photograph, or become a container of images. Identity can also serve as a surrounding framework for individual show graphics.
